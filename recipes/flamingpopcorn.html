Flaming Popcorn



A crunchy, finger licking bowl of salty flaming goodness!

This recipe was hand-crafted by Hades himself, then passed along to Jack & Ari to serve the public in hopes of leaving everyone flamed! This salty, buttery, flaming combo can only be compared to crack-cocaine itself!

Ingredients

  • Organic white popcorn
  • One bag of Flaming Hot Cheetos
  • A full stick of Kerrygolds Butter
  • Kosher salt
  • Olive oil

How to make!

  1. First, take a chili sized pot and pour just enough olive oil in to cover the bottom of the pot
  2. Pour popcorn seeds in pan until you can't see the bottom, and then pour some more
  3. Turn that fucker up to like 8 or Hi and wait for the magic
  4. As it pops, shake the pot so that the seeds continuously fall to the bottom for consistent poppage
  5. When pops are seperated by about 2.5 seconds, youll know its done
  6. Add salt with each half pour of Butter
  7. Melt your butter and pour half on, shake pot, then pour other half
  8. Get a viking sized wooden bowl, and pour in popcorn
  9. Add full bag of Flaming Hot Cheetos
  10. Fuck yourself
  11. Enjoy!
